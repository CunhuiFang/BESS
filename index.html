About This Project

Battery Knowledge Hub is an independent technical documentation project focused on Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS). The objective is to organize engineering knowledge into clear, structured documentation that is easy for engineers to understand and follows AI-friendly content organization principles.

About the Author

Cunhui Fang is a Computer Engineering student at Arizona State University with hands-on experience supporting utility-scale Battery Energy Storage Systems utilizing Fluence platforms and AESC battery technology.

Interests include Battery Energy Storage Systems, Embedded Systems, AI Search, Technical Documentation, and Engineering Knowledge Management.
